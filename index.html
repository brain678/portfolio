Meet Olayemi Ogunleye

Olayemi Ogunleye is currently a postgraduate student at the University of Worcester, UK, pursuing a course in International Business & Management Studies. He holds the position of Vice President for Student’s Activities and is proud to be Nigerian, with roots in Lagos. His slogan is "Equity and Fairness!" and his favorite quote is, "When you stop learning, you start dying."

Vision

My Action Plan For progress

1. Empowering Student-Driven Change: I will prioritize and support student-led initiatives that directly improve the student experience.

2. Boosting Student Engagement: I will actively promote and facilitate student involvement in Students' Union extracurricular activities, adhering to University regulations.

3. Partnering for Success: I will work constructively with Union executives to achieve our collective objectives.

4. Driving Policy That Matters: I will collaborate with the Union, the University, and students to create and implement impactful policies that serve all stakeholders.

My Commitment to Enriching Student Life

I'm running because I believe in enhancing the student experience here in Worcester. My vision is to create a more inclusive and actively engaged student community. My extensive background in leadership, entrepreneurship, and management, coupled with my experience as a former Student Union Vice President and my current MBA in Marketing, equips me to deliver real results. For over 15 years, l've honed my skills in leading organizations and driving success, and I'm eager to bring that experience to serve you.
